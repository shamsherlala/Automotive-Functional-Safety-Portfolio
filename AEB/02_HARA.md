# 02 — Hazard Analysis and Risk Assessment (HARA)
**Document ID:** AEB-HARA-002
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 07.07.2026
**Author:** Shamsher Lala
**Status:** Draft

---

## 1. Operating Scenarios
- Rainy or adverse weather conditions
- High speed highway driving (>80 km/h)
- Dirty or obstructed sensors (mud, snow, dust)
- Urban driving (crossings, traffic lights, pedestrians)

---

## 2. Hazardous Events

| ID | Hazardous Event | S | E | C | ASIL |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| HE1 | AEB fails to brake due to obstructed sensor, leading to collision with objects in muddy conditions | S3 | E2 | C3 | B |
| HE2 | AEB applies brakes due to obstructed sensor, leading to rear collision from following vehicles | S3 | E2 | C3 | B |
| HE3 | AEB fails to brake in urban driving, leading to collision with pedestrian | S3 | E3 | C1 | A |

---

## 3. Parameter Definitions
- **S3** — Life threatening or fatal injuries
- **E2** — Low probability of exposure
- **E3** — Medium probability of exposure
- **C1** — Controllable by most drivers
- **C3** — Difficult to control or uncontrollable
